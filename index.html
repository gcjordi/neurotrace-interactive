About the project

NeuroTrace Interactive explores how AI systems can be made more understandable through visual metaphors. It is designed for AI safety, transparency, and responsible AI communication without revealing hidden prompts, security logic, private evaluations, or proprietary methods.

This project is inspired by research in AI safety, cognitive cybersecurity, and interpretability-oriented interfaces.
